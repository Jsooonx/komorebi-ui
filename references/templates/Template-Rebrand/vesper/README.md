# Vesper Atelier

Vesper Atelier is a premium spatial, interior, and object design template. It is a full dark-mode rebrand of `Lovable-Design/Pallet-Ross`, maintaining the original's high-fidelity scroll card interaction while applying an elevated architectural mood.

## Design Concept

- **Aesthetic**: Deep dark tones (obsidian, rich charcoal slate), brushed warm brass, and ivory accents.
- **Typography**: Editorial serif typography for headings (Playfair Display) combined with clean geometric monospace for material annotations.
- **Material Showcase**: The scrollable cards display a curated curation of brutalist architecture, travertine surfaces, cast bronze fixtures, and shadow play studies.
- **Interactive Experience**: Card fan-out, cascade, and stack animation linked directly to page scroll.
